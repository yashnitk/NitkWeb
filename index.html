Research Identity

Dr. Kashyap’s work lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, renewable energy systems, smart grids, and airborne wind energy. His research integrates machine learning, remote sensing, numerical modelling, real-time simulation, and prototype-based validation for intelligent and sustainable energy systems.
